Project

The primary objectives of this performance testing are:

  • Measure Brake Power (BP), Thermal Efficiency, Specific Fuel Consumption (SFC), and emissions.
  • Assess engine behavior under varying loads and speeds.

Key Parameters

The following parameters are measured during the test:

  • Speed (RPM) using a tachometer.
  • Torque with a dynamometer.
  • Fuel consumption using a flow meter.
  • Exhaust gases using a gas analyzer.

Key Metrics

The key metrics calculated during the test are:

  • Brake Power
  • Thermal Efficiency
  • Specific Fuel Consumption

Procedure

  • Mount engine on a test bed with fuel, cooling, and exhaust systems connected.
  • Operate at various loads and speeds, recording speed, torque, fuel consumption, and emissions.
  • Calculate metrics and plot graphs (e.g., BP vs. RPM, SFC vs. RPM).
  • Equipment used during the test includes a dynamometer, fuel flow meter, and tachometer.
